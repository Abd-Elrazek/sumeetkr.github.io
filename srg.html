Security Reading Group

Security Reading Group meets almost every week on Tuesday from 12:30pm to 1:30pm in 606 Soda Hall. We eat and chat for the first 15 minutes, and the presenter presents or leads discussion for the next 45. Please do not hesitate to contact me with questions, comments, or concerns about the group.

If you attend Security Reading Group regularly, please volunteer to present and get food at least once each semester. Please sign up on the sheet that I bring to each meeting.

Instructions for presenters

You are welcome to present your own work or somebody else's. The most common strategy seems to be to choose a paper that interests you from a recent conference. Many of the papers we discuss come from CCS, NDSS, Oakland, or USENIX Security, but anything related to security that you find exciting is appropriate.

You are responsible for leading the discussion. This may include making sure people stay on topic, or reminding someone to give everyone else a chance to speak. Do not be afraid to do either of these things. If you are giving a practice talk and would not like to be interrupted, please tell everyone this before beginning your talk.

Instructions for getting food

Please take the following things into consideration:
  • Please plan to feed about 14 people, unless I tell you otherwise.
  • Please provide beverages.
  • Please do not spend more than $10 per person.
  • Please make sure the food arrives on time. The most reliable way to do this is to plan for it to be there 10 minutes ahead of time.
  • Below is a list of food allergies/aversions and how many people have them. Please plan accordingly. If you get pizza, please also get salad for 2 to 3 people.

    • Mayonnaise (1)
    • Dairy (1)
    • Cilantro (1)
    • Mushrooms (1)
  • You will be reimbursed for the food and beverages you purchase. Please bring your receipt(s) to Angie Abbatecola, who sits in 784 Soda Hall. She will help you fill out the right form to get your money back.
