Background

I am a PhD student in the Institute for Software Research at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am advised by Prof. Kathleen M. Carley. Before joininig CMU, I have worked at various companies including (Symantec , Talentica , and Intergraph ) as a Software Engineer.
